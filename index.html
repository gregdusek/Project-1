Rules Of The Game.

Player one will start the game.

Two players compete against one another catching fish.

Each player will cast till they reel in 3 fish.

The total weight of each fish is added together.

The player with the heavier weight of fish wins the game.

A player may hook a fish that's unable to be reeled in.

That's why they call it fishing and not catching.

Have fun!
